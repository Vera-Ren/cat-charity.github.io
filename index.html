🔎 Where Your Donations Go

  • Cat Food & Supplements: Providing daily essential healthy food to ensure the cats stay strong.
  • Medical Aid: Covering medical expenses for injured or sick cats, including vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and routine check-ups.
  • Shelter Construction & Maintenance: Improving the living environment for cats and providing warm, comfortable accommodations.
  • Adoption Promotion: Organizing adoption events to help cats find their forever homes.
  • Emergency Reserve: Responding to unforeseen circumstances and urgent rescues.
