Our Story

Hello! I'm Aiko, an ordinary cat lover. My story began with a little cat named 'Peanut'. Three years ago, on a cold rainy night, I found Peanut huddled in a corner, completely drenched. She was emaciated and her eyes were full of fear. At that moment, I decided to take her home and give her a warm sanctuary.

After recovering, Peanut became lively and adorable, returning my love in her own unique way every day. Her arrival completely changed my life and made me realize that countless stray cats, just like Peanut, needed help. This strong sense of mission drove me to create this platform, hoping to gather more love and support for these homeless little lives.

We believe every life deserves to be treated with kindness. Through this website, we hope to share our story, connect with more like-minded friends, and together provide food, medical care, and homes for stray cats. Thank you for visiting, we look forward to shining a light of hope for cats with you!
